Nature guide
Hans and Bo Tonnesen have been nature guides for many years and still are, in addition, they are both board members at the Danish Nature Conservation Society. Both Hans and Bo have a lot of interest in the wildlife there is to be found down here by tønder area b.la. the bird life out by the wading, where they have also got a "Nature School" up and stand in cooperation with Tønder municipality, both Hans and Bo have great love for nature and that is also why they choose to take it to the next step where they share their knowledge. With Hans and Bo's many years of experience as a supervisor, there is no reason to be in doubt, they are more than willing to learn from them and they will be hugely happy if they could teach you something new, so leave a message or a call if you want to come outside and experience the nature waiting for you!
